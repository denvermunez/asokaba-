Fitness refers to a state of health and well-being that results from regular physical activity, proper nutrition, and adequate rest. It encompasses various components including cardiovascular endurance, muscular strength, flexibility, and body composition. Maintaining fitness helps improve energy levels, enhances mental clarity, boosts the immune system, and reduces the risk of chronic diseases such as diabetes, heart disease, and obesity. Fitness is not just about appearance it's about cultivating a strong, balanced body and mind to support a healthy and active lifestyle.
